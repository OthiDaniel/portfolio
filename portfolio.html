About Me

Hello.
Welcome to my world.

Until recently I was a kid at heart until I got a close proximity to a plane. I grew up all of a sudden, got tired of being a big baby and started working to shape my life.

I never knew I could be a programmer until I landed in Moringa school. Being a good basketball player in Kenya is hard, I mean you put in all the hard work in high school only to end nowhere in premier basketball as Kenya's Basketball Federation is overrun by corrupt officials. I therefore took it upon myself to do something about it. It is my dream that some day in future the NBA will be picking players from Kenya and it is upon me to make this happen.
My aim is to build a working website and log every player in learning institutions and clubs all over the country. This way anyone anywhere can view the player statistics, keep track on them and even deploy scouts to Kenya. I will for sure make this happen and watch our kids fly abroad to play in the biggest court in the world. So help me God.
